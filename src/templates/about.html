About Polymath

We are a strategic storytelling agency advancing the good with visionary organizations and brands.

What does Polymath do? We are your one-stop creative partner for communications and thinking.

Polymath exists to find and amplify your voice. From ideation to implementation, we tell stories and develop strategies that realize your goals and resonate with your audiences.

Since 2013, more than 70 businesses, brands, and nonprofits have entrusted us to understand their dreams, design their platforms, and deploy their campaigns. When our skills combine with your cause, the results move movements.

Who is Polymath?

Our team of problem-solving storytellers—led by founder Dr. Timothy Dalrymple—represents a unique combination of communicators and creatives, teachers and technologists, producers and PhDs, with experience in the business, religion, academic, and entertainment sectors. …No rocket scientists, though*.
